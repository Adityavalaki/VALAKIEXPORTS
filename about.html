About Valaki

Rooted in farming. Built for export.

Valaki Exports Co. is part of the Valaki Brothers group — large-scale farmers by inheritance in Mahuva, India's primary belt for high-pungency onion and garlic.

We control the chain end to end: seed and crop nutrition, harvest, dehydration and milling at our Euro Foods processing plant, and direct container export.

One family, one accountable line from field to port — serving food manufacturers, importers and private-label brands worldwide.

From Farm to Port

Six controlled stages.

01

Raw selection

Contract-farm onion, garlic and spices, raised under Good Agricultural Practices.

02

Wash & sort

Mechanical wash, peel and manual inspection remove defects and foreign matter.

03

Dehydration

Low-temperature belt drying keeps essential oils, colour and nutrition intact.

04

Mill & sortex

Milled to mesh, then magnet, metal-detector and optical colour sorting.

05

Hygienic pack

Poly-lined cartons and multi-wall bags — 20 kg standard or to your spec.

06

Seal & ship

Loaded at our warehouse under customs seal, dispatched via Mundra & Pipavav.
